What We Do

Full-Service Commercial Truck Repair

We handle everything from routine maintenance to major overhauls. If it has an engine and hauls freight, we fix it.

Roadside & Mobile Repair

Broke down on I-57, I-80, or anywhere in Chicago's south suburbs? We come to you with the tools to get you moving. Fast response, real repairs — not just band-aids. Available for after-hours emergencies.

Diesel Engine Repair & Overhaul

Complete diesel engine diagnostics, repair, and rebuild services for all major makes — Cummins, Detroit, PACCAR, Volvo, Navistar. From turbo failures to complete overhauls, we do it right.

Transmission & Drivetrain

Manual and automatic transmission service, clutch replacement, driveshaft repair, differential service, and U-joint replacement. We keep your drivetrain solid.

Brakes, Drums & Suspension

Air brakes, drum brakes, disc brakes, slack adjusters, brake chambers, shocks, leaf springs, and air ride suspension. Don't risk a violation — or worse. We keep you stopping safe.

Computer Diagnostics

Full electronic diagnostics on all major truck systems — engine, transmission, ABS, aftertreatment/DPF. We read and clear fault codes so you know exactly what's wrong before spending a dime.

Oil Change & Preventative Maintenance

Quick, thorough oil changes and full PM services to keep your truck healthy and your warranty valid. We check fluids, filters, belts, hoses, and more — catching problems before they become breakdowns.

Tires: Steer, Drive & Trailer

We stock steer, drive, and trailer tires in sizes you actually need. Mounting, balancing, alignment checks, and flat repair. No waiting around for parts — we keep common sizes in stock.

Electrical & Lighting

Wiring harness repair, alternator and starter service, battery testing and replacement, headlight and marker light repair. We trace and fix electrical gremlins that other shops can't figure out.

HVAC & Climate Control

AC recharge and repair, heater core service, blower motor replacement. Stay comfortable in the cab year-round. We service all major makes and systems.

Fuel System Service

Fuel injector cleaning and replacement, fuel pump service, fuel filter replacement, and fuel system diagnostics. Restore power and fuel economy.

DPF & Aftertreatment Service

DPF cleaning, regen issues, DEF system diagnostics, SCR catalyst service, and EGR repair. Keep your emissions systems running clean and avoid costly fines.

DOT Inspections

Annual DOT inspections done right. Honest pass/fail — no upselling, no surprises. We inspect trucks, trailers, and buses. Stay compliant and stay on the road.

Fleet Maintenance Programs

Scheduled PM programs, priority service lanes, detailed service records, and a single shop that knows your trucks inside and out. Built for fleet owners and operators who demand reliability.
