ABOUT CONVOY

Built for Truckers, by Truckers

Our Story

We've Been in the Cab

Convoy Truck Repair didn't start in a boardroom. It started on the highway. Our founder Thomas spent over 20 years as an owner-operator, logging millions of miles across the country. He knows what it's like to break down at 2 AM, to get overcharged by a shop that sees an out-of-town plate, and to lose a load because nobody could fix the truck fast enough.

That frustration became the blueprint for Convoy. We opened our doors in Matteson, IL — right off I-57 Exit 340 — with a simple mission: treat every trucker the way we wanted to be treated when we were behind the wheel.

Today, we've serviced over 10,000 trucks and built a reputation for honest work, fair prices, and fast turnaround. We're still trucker-owned, still hands-on, and still obsessed with getting you back on the road.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

The Road Years

Thomas starts his career as an owner-operator, driving OTR routes across the country. Learns firsthand what truckers need — and what most repair shops get wrong.

2015 — Convoy Opens

Convoy Truck Repair opens at 5501 Adams St in Matteson, IL. Two bays, a handful of tools, and a commitment to doing things differently.

2017 — Mobile Service Launches

We add roadside and mobile repair service, bringing the shop to broken-down trucks along I-57 and Chicago's south suburbs.

2019 — Fleet Programs Begin

Local fleet operators start asking for scheduled maintenance programs. We build a system to track, schedule, and manage entire fleets.

2022 — Expanded Facility

Growing demand leads to facility expansion — more bays, more equipment, and a bigger parts inventory to reduce wait times.

Today — 10,000+ Trucks Serviced

We've grown from a two-bay shop to a full-service commercial truck repair facility, but the mission hasn't changed. Honest work, fair prices, fast turnaround.
